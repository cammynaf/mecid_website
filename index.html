About

Who we are

Communities face disability exclusion, limited access to education, unemployment, and inadequate psychosocial support. MECID responds by strengthening skills, opportunities, and community systems that protect and include everyone.

Our mission

To promote inclusive education, social empowerment, and sustainable livelihoods for vulnerable individuals and families.

Who we serve

  • Persons with disabilities (especially out-of-school youth)
  • Mothers and caregivers of children with disabilities
  • Young mothers
  • Survivors and individuals at risk of SGBV
